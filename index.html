Research Interests

My research focuses on the mathematical modeling of tumor evolution and therapeutic response through frameworks such as branching processes, ordinary differential equations (ODEs), and stochastic differential equations (SDEs). I employ probabilistic and statistical methods to theoretically analyze these models, uncover intratumoral heterogeneity, evaluate therapeutic implications, and estimate key parameters of tumor dynamics.
